Orbit is a one-tap arcade game with gravity-based orbital mechanics.

Tap to release your dot from its orbit. Gravity pulls it toward the next circle — time your release to arc into the catch zone. Chain perfect shots to build your multiplier.

<b>How to Play</b>
• Tap to release the dot tangentially from its orbit
• Gravity wells pull the dot toward nearby circles
• Land in the catch zone to score
• Perfect shots (aimed straight at the circle) build a multiplier streak
• Miss the circle or run out of time — game over

<b>Features</b>
• Gravity-based orbital mechanics with satisfying curved arcs
• Arcade-style top 10 leaderboard with 3-character initials
• Synthesized retro sounds with rising pitch on perfect streaks
• CRT scanlines, neon glow, vector aesthetic
• Attract mode with auto-play demo
• OLED-friendly pure black background
• Scales to any screen size (foldable-friendly)
• 60fps rendering

<b>Privacy</b>
No permissions required. No ads. No tracking. No internet access. Your data stays on your device.
